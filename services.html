What we do

Transformation is not a set of disconnected services — it's a structured journey from complexity to clarity, and from clarity to execution.

Operating model clarity & transformation strategy

Most organisations don't have a strategy problem. They have an alignment problem — where strategy, governance, and execution exist in separate conversations and never fully connect.

We design operating models that give leadership a single, coherent system to work from.

What this looks like in practice

  • Designing operating models that create clarity across complex, multi-site organisations
  • Aligning strategy, governance, and execution into a single coherent framework
  • Prioritising transformation initiatives based on measurable value creation
  • Restructuring decision rights so the right people own the right outcomes
  • Building accountability structures that hold without constant management overhead

Who this is for

Leadership teams that are well-resourced and well-intentioned, but find that results are inconsistent and transformation isn't sticking.

Outcomes

Structural clarity A single operating model that connects strategy to daily delivery
Aligned execution Teams working from the same priorities, not competing ones
Durable governance Accountability that functions without constant intervention

Operational performance & process improvement

Operational friction is expensive and largely invisible. Teams adapt around it, workarounds become standard practice, and what looks like a people problem is usually a systems problem.

We find the friction, quantify it, and remove it — permanently.

What this looks like in practice

  • Identifying and removing friction across core operational processes
  • Redesigning workflows to eliminate duplication, delays, and handover failures
  • Building performance measurement systems that surface problems early
  • Improving speed, efficiency, and cost performance across operations
  • Embedding continuous improvement into normal operating rhythms

Who this is for

Operations leaders who know something isn't working — costs are higher than they should be, cycle times are too long, or results are inconsistent — but aren't sure exactly where the problem lives.

Outcomes

Faster throughput Reduced cycle times and fewer handover failures
Lower cost to serve Waste and duplication removed, not just reduced
Visible performance Clear metrics that tell you when things drift before they break

Digital enablement & workflow automation

Automation is valuable when it removes genuinely manual, error-prone work. Most organisations have more of this than they realise — buried in email chains, spreadsheets, and manual approval processes.

We identify where automation creates real ROI and build it so it holds.

What this looks like in practice

  • Automating manual, repetitive processes using Power Platform and connected tooling
  • Building reporting and visibility systems that support real-time decision-making
  • Integrating disconnected tools and platforms to improve information flow
  • Designing digital workflows that are scalable, maintainable, and user-adopted
  • Supporting data infrastructure and BI capability uplift

Who this is for

Organisations where manual processes are constraining growth or accuracy — where a significant proportion of team time is consumed by work that a well-designed system could do instead.

Outcomes

Capacity recovered Manual effort removed so teams focus on higher-value work
Accuracy improved Automated processes eliminate the error rate of manual handling
Decisions supported Leaders have the visibility they need, when they need it

Procurement & vendor optimisation

Procurement is often the last function to be treated as strategic. The result is slow cycle times, fragmented vendor relationships, and cost structures that are difficult to interrogate or control.

We redesign procurement into a function that actively creates value.

What this looks like in practice

  • Reducing procurement cycle times without compromising compliance or quality
  • Consolidating and optimising vendor ecosystems to improve performance and accountability
  • Improving cost visibility and building structural cost reduction programs
  • Redesigning procurement governance to support faster, better decisions
  • Negotiating improved commercial terms and vendor performance frameworks

Who this is for

Organisations where procurement is a bottleneck — where decisions are slow, costs are opaque, or vendor relationships are consuming disproportionate management time.

Outcomes

Faster cycles Procurement timelines measured in weeks, not months
Better commercial terms Structured negotiation that captures value systematically
Strategic function Procurement that creates competitive advantage, not just compliance

How we engage

We work in three engagement modes depending on where you are and what you need. Most engagements start with a diagnostic before we agree on scope.

Advisory

Structured diagnostic and transformation planning for leadership teams who need clarity before committing to a program. Typically 2–6 weeks. Ends with a clear picture of what to fix and in what order.

Delivery

End-to-end execution support from design through to embedded performance. We stay until the change holds. Typically 3–12 months depending on scope.

Embedded

Our people inside your operation, driving improvement from the ground up alongside your team. Suited to environments with high operational complexity or significant time pressure.

Every engagement starts with clarity on the problem before we agree on the solution.
