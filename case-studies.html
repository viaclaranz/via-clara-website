Trans-Tasman Fleet Operating Model Transformation

Operations Transformation Operating Model Design Fleet Operations

The Challenge

A large Australasian organisation faced fragmented fleet operations across New Zealand and Australia, resulting in:

  • Inconsistent regional operating practices
  • High fleet downtime and low asset utilisation
  • Limited operational visibility for leadership
  • Slow turnaround across maintenance and servicing

Approach

  • Designed and implemented a unified trans-Tasman operating model
  • Standardised maintenance, compliance, and asset workflows
  • Aligned operations, procurement, and finance functions
  • Embedded continuous improvement into operational delivery

The Results

25% Permanent Workforce Reduction
80%+ Downtime Reduction
16,000+ Assets Managed
AU/NZ Integrated Operations
